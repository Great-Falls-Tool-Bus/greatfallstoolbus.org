discuss@latoolb.us is the public board for the Great Falls Tool Bus: an open, readable place to talk tools and projects with your neighbors.
An open place to talk
It is the everyday back-and-forth of a shared tool library: what to build, how a repair went, which tool did the job, who is up to what around Lewiston-Auburn.
Borrowing a tool is a separate, personal path: the board is for talking, access is for doing. To use the bus, start with how access works.
